Memorial/Sympathy Flowers

B’s Garden Treasures will preserve your loved one's flowers. The flowers will be carefully taken apart, pressed flat – using modern pressing techniques to remove the moisture – preserved and creatively reconstructed and arranged into framed art which can be displayed in your home and cherished for years to come. You will be involved in all design decisions and will have the opportunity to review and approve the design concept before it is finalized.

Your special flowers will be vacuum sealed and professionally framed. Acid-free products and UV-protective glass are used to ensure the lasting quality of your heirloom.

Full Memorial/Sympathy Floral Arrangement

The entire floral arrangement will be pressed and preserved and creatively arranged. The flowers may be arranged around a photograph of your loved one, verse, poem, funeral card, eulogy, etc. to create a sentimental and unique heirloom. Choice of frames and matting. Unused flowers can be used for additional keepsakes or returned to the family.

Other Keepsakes

B's Garden Treasures can create additional small keepsakes from the memorial/sympathy flowers. Examples include glass ornaments, pendants, laminated cards, digital copies of the floral arrangement, etc. These make great keepsakes for family members. Contact B's Garden Treasures for pricing.

How to Care for Your Flowers and Miscellaneous

Keep the flowers as fresh as possible, until you can get them to B’s Garden Treasures. Put them in a cool place, with their stems in water. Do not put water on the flower petals. You can place the flowers in the refrigerator but DO NOT FREEZE! For best results, get them to B’s Garden Treasures within 1-3 days. Please contact B’s Garden Treasures at info@BGTreasures.com as soon as possible to make sure I am available to preserve your flowers. If I am not available, I may be able to refer you to another pressed flower artist.

A non-refundable $100 deposit is required upon receipt of your flowers. The remainder is due upon shipping or receipt.

B’s Garden Treasures reserves the right to refuse flowers which are of poor quality. You will be contacted within 2-4 weeks to discuss your design preferences. The order will be finished and ready for your enjoyment within 6-12 weeks.
